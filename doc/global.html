InterpreterMethodResult

The result of an external:InterpreterObject#interpreterMethod. This will either be some kind of container for the results of the parts of the interpreter method, or a user defined result of an interpretation, which may have any type. The purpose of this type definition is to explain the values that are returned by interpreter methods in type dependent containers, if they have no interpretation, or passed as arguments to their interpretation, if they have one.
Type:
  • *

part

Many external:InterpreterObject#interpreterMethods are defined in terms of other interpreter methods by constructing them with the interpreterMethodNames of those methods. Then the inner methods will be referred to as parts of the outer method. Any regular expressions in the definition of an interpreter method are not counted among its parts.
